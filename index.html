Visage / Resident Evil Inspired scene


Inspired by both Visage and Resident Evil, at first the scene was only meant to be set in the basement, but I wanted to work on my lighting, especially a well lit room. An environment made using modular assets made in Blender. Textured both in Blender and Substance Painter. Lighting and Rendering along with post processing made in Unreal Engine 4.



Here are the different modular assets made in Blender. The Bankers Lamp isn't my model though. "Bankers Lamp" (https://skfb.ly/orsMy) by Scar56 is licensed under Creative Commons Attribution (http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
